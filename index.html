Jordan B. Peterson

Lecturer, Educator, and Clinical Psychologist

  • 1962 - Dr. Peterson was born in Fairview, Alberta.
  • 1982 - Earns a B.A. in Political Science from the University of Alberta
  • 1984 - Earns a B.A. in Psychology from the University of Alberta.
  • 1991 - Earns his P.hD in Clinical Psychology from McGill University.
  • 1993 - 1997 - Taught at Harvard University.
  • 1997 - Becomes full professor at the University of Toronto.
  • 2016 - Posts controversial videos criticizing Bill C-16.
